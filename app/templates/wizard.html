homelab-helper

First-time setup — let's configure it for your environment.
Environment scan
We scanned your Docker environment to pre-populate settings where possible. Everything can be adjusted in the next steps.
Homelab identity
A display name shown in the app header.
Domains
The domains you own. The first entry is the default when generating compose snippets. Sandbox URLs use the first domain (e.g. try-recipes-a1b2.example.com).
At least one domain is required.
Traefik
homelab-helper generates Traefik labels for new services. Configure the network and routing settings that match your Traefik setup.
Network, entrypoint, and cert resolver are required.
Integrations
Enable optional integrations. homelab-helper will generate the relevant labels and YAML snippets for anything you turn on.
Review & create
This is the config that will be written to config.yaml. You can edit it at any time after setup.
